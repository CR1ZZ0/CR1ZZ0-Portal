How to order a commission?


Procedure

Last Updated: May 28, 2025

Introduction

Please read this Procedure as they govern the cooperation by Me (Cr1zz0) and the Client (You).
Read this Thoroughly and make sure to follow these guidelines.



Step 1

Visit my Github site and click on the commission selection menu.

Step 2

Navigate and choose the item you wanna get.

Step 3

Copy the Order Code of the item you wanna get or Note the name.

Step 4

Approach me on any of my sites. (DM pls, Comments will automatically deleted due to safety)

Step 5

State which item you want, and paste your Order Code.

Step 6

Send your references and specify the details.

Step 7

Wait for me to respond and to negotiate the payments.

Step 8

If it's a deal, then send to me the payments and it's proof of purchase (Kofi screenshot or Invoice).
(work starts after i get the money)

Step 9

If you want to set deadlines, pay extra.

Step 10

During the production, stay in contact with me i may need clarifications. Wait patiently,
Your name will be on the Queue line on the github site so you can track our progress.

Step 11

Finally, will send a full file. This generally would be a .rar file sooo you need to extract it later.
But if you want it just .png then it's fine.
